# WebAstros Agency - Value-First Business Flow

This document outlines our highly converting "Value-First" strategy for digital agency client acquisition. Instead of traditional cold-calling, we leverage rapid prototyping to build trust and prove competence upfront.

## 1. Prospect Discovery (Lead Generation)
Using the internal **Lead Gen Pro** dashboard, we search for hyper-local businesses and filter for high-conversion candidates.

**Target Criteria:**
- High Google Review rating but **No Website** tagged.
- Outdated websites that are completely inaccessible on mobile phones.
- Niche businesses missing massive revenue opportunities (e.g., Restaurants without WhatsApp ordering or reservations).

## 2. Pre-Outreach Production (Value Creation)
Instead of reaching out with empty promises, we invest 15 minutes to generate tangible proof using templates.

**Workflow:**
- Rapidly deploy highly-optimized "Master Templates" designed via Stitch/Antigravity for specific verticals.
- Swap out colors, logos, and insert AI-generated placeholder copy relevant to the business.
- Inject specific functional hooks (e.g., a shiny "Order on WhatsApp" button).
- Deploy to a clean staging link.

## 3. The Teaser Outreach
Contact the business owner via Phone, Email, or In-Person visit, leaning entirely on the value created.

**Demonstration Script Angle:**
> "Hi [Name], I'm a local developer. I noticed your business didn't have an easy way for customers to view your services online, so I actually went ahead and built a free prototype website for [Business Name] over the weekend. Here's a private link to view it. Let me know if you'd like to use it!"

## 4. Onboarding & Execution
Once the client engages and experiences the "wow factor", we convert them to a paid contract.

**Hand-off & Fulfillment:**
- Navigate to the Lead Gen Pro dashboard and mark the lead as "Won Deal".
- Conduct a rapid discovery call to capture correct content, high-resolution imagery, and exact services.
- Finalize the Next.js/React App prototype or hook it into the main WordPress infrastructure.
- Deliver the final product and transition the client onto a monthly hosting/maintenance retainer.
